## 3. Indie Hackers Post

**Title:**
> I built a Shopify app that makes your products visible to ChatGPT and Claude. Score Guarantee in writing.

**Body:**
> 4 months ago a friend running a skincare brand told me her ChatGPT
> traffic was zero. Her Shopify SEO was fine. Her Google rank was
> decent. But when she typed "best hydrating serum under $50" into
> ChatGPT, her product never came up.
>
> I dug in. The issue wasn't quality — her serum is great. The issue
> was that ChatGPT had no clean signal to grab. Title was 4 words.
> No alt text. Metafields blank. Description was 80 words of "premium
> luxury feel". An AI agent literally had nothing to recommend her on.
>
> So I started building **AI Catalog Score**: a Shopify app that
> reads your catalog the same way ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Mistral,
> and DeepSeek do — and rewrites what's broken.
>
> **What it does**
>
> - Scores every product across 8 dimensions an LLM actually uses
>   (title structure, description factual density, alt text coverage,
>   variant attributes, metafields, Shopify category, SEO, pricing
>   signals)
> - One-click "Maximize" runs Claude on a single product and writes
>   the rewrites directly into Shopify with 30-day undo
> - Causal A/B testing on every fix batch (Difference-in-Differences,
>   Welch's t-test, p-values reported)
> - Score Guarantee in Terms of Service v1.0: +10 points in 30 days or
>   the month auto-refunds
>
> **Why this is hard**
>
> The temptation is to write "AI-ready" copy. We did that for the
> first month. It was crap. Claude needs to be steered by the AUDIT —
> if the issue is "title missing product type" the fix needs to add
> the product type, not just sound good.
>
> So we built a per-criterion pre-flight check: each rewrite is
> simulated against the audit BEFORE being applied. If scoreTitle
> doesn't improve, the title rewrite is skipped. No regressions, no
> "AI made it worse" moments.
>
> **The dataset**
>
> The methodology is calibrated on 270k+ real AI agent captures
> (we run synthetic shopping queries across 6 agents daily and store
> what gets recommended). Methodology + dataset are open source CC0:
> github.com/commerce-agentic
>
> **Pricing**
>
> Free up to 15 SKUs forever.
> Growth $49/mo, Pro $149/mo, Performance $399/mo OR 5% of measured
> AI revenue uplift capped $5k/mo. The usage tier is genuinely
> zero-base — if our work doesn't lift revenue, we earn nothing.
>
> **Status**
>
> Live at aicatalogscore.com. Free install via Shopify App Store
> (currently in review). First merchant beta showed +30 points avg
> score lift on 14 skincare SKUs, projected +$30,870/mo revenue
> recovery.
>

> check so rewrites can't regress the score.

>
> 1. **Per-criterion pre-flight.** Each Claude rewrite is simulated
>    against the audit on a hypothetical product BEFORE it's written
>    to Shopify. We gate on the sub-score the rewrite targets (e.g.
>    title rewrite → scoreTitle), not the total — so a rewrite that
>    nails a structural fix (productType insertion) but costs 1pt on
>    length still ships.

> 2. **Causal A/B with p-values.** Most "AI revenue uplift" claims are
>    observational. We use Difference-in-Differences with Welch's

>    we count it as a "real" lift.
